Our Services

Hair Replacement

Hair Replacement can restore hair to any desired style, and is ideal for those who are in the advanced stages of baldness.

Hair Bonding

Hair bonding is short term detachable approach. Special adhesives are used to bond a section of wefted hair/ hair strands to your natural hair.

Hair Weaving

100% non-surgical hair weaving is the science of addition of hair to the scalp by the weaving or braiding of human or synthetic hair.

Hair Wigs

Hair fixing is a painless hair restoration procedure with no side effects and gives you absolute natural looking hair which give your hair look back.

Hair Fixing

Hair fixing is an art of providing natural looking hair to bald people by attaching hair system.

Hair Clipping

This technique is the least permanent and can be very effective without the negative things associated with glue extensions.
